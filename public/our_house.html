• We bought a house!

    15 February, 2020

    We dropped off a check to Pulte and locked in our choices for a new Home in Hall Lake! We choose to go with the Roslynn Layout. The lot we choose is far enough out that we get to customize a lot of aspects of the house. I included a sample floor plan of some of the structural options we chose.

    We opted to go with a 3 car garage instead of a 4th bedroom, this should give use more storage space as a well as a workshop like area for Josh. We think there should still have pleanty of space for guests!

    We also splurged on the outdoor fireplace. We gave this one a lot of thought and figured while we both like a real wood fire the overhead of starting one and safely dealing with it would mean it would probably not happen as often as we'd like. We think the gas fireplace which turns on with a flip of a switch will help that.

    In the kitchen we upgraded the kitchen island to a larger version. It adds some extra storage, seating, and adds a bit more countertop space.

    Upstairs we have not changed too much. It's not shown in the floor plan but we opted to upgrade our Laundry room to have a gas stub for a Gas Dryer (cheaper utilties in long run, shorter dry times, and less static are some benefits) as well got a sink pre-plumbed and Josh will install it himself in the future.

    In the Owner's suite we decided to upgrade the bathroom shower a bit. We went for walkin framed shower. There is a rain fall shower head as well as a normal one. I wish we had pictures to share, but for now you will just have to trust us that it was hard to pass up!

    We did it! Look for more of these shots as the house is built!

  • Our Journey Begins!

    1 February, 2020

    Olivia and myself had been dreaming about a house for a while, and when 2020 rolled around we resolved ourselves to buy a house. Initially we had been looking exclusively within Seattle and had found a lot of properties we liked in the West Seattle area and some in nearby South Seattle neighborhoods.

    We learned a lot! Especially about what we really cared about and we both preferred newer construction to a project home. A lot of older properties still sell for (in my opinion) absurdly high prices in Seattle, it was very hard to rationalize the costs of a house that still required a lot money and time when newer homes were selling at the similar prices.

    On February 8th 2020, we saw a community North of Seattle called Hall Lake built by Pulte Homes. The homes were beautiful and we saw something that made us pause awant to stop looking. Although... the first time we went looking for it, we got lost and couldn't find it 😆
